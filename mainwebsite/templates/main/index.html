Diversity and Pillars

In Careers:
Through their ambition, open-mindness, and hard work, our brothers excel in all areas of business, including entrepreneurship, technology, marketing, consulting, and investment banking, to name a few. Whether you want to work in Silicon Valley or on Wall Street, for an innovative start-up or a Fortune 100 company, Delta Sigma Pi provides the network, resources, and mentorship necessary for you to land your desired job. All you have to answer is, “What is my dream job and company?”
